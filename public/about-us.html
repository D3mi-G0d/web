Welcome

#HI there!

SNU School of Engineering has been established with a vision to lead in computer science education in research. We aspire to provide practical use of computer science and its technology among the students who will stand as brand ambassadors for our university in the digitalized, professional platform. With state-of-the-art facilities, industry-academia interface, professional guidance, industry-integrated specialized courses, industrial visits/seminars and so on, we aim to imbibe our students with the technological excellence.

Dr. Chandan Kumar Bhattacharyya

Dean at Sister Nivedita University
Dr. Bhattacharyya earned his degree in B. Sc. Honors in Physics in the year 1989. Then he pursued B. Tech and M. Tech degree in Computer Science & Engg from the University of Calcutta in the year 1992 and 1994 respectively. He then joined Electronics Research & Development Center of India (ER&DCI), Calcutta as Scientific Officer where he served approximately 10 years and left in the year 2003 as Sr. Scientific Officer. During his stay in Industry, Prof. Bhattacharyya earned his MBA degree in Systems as topper from IISW&BM in 2001. He also completed his PhD degree in Computer Science & Engg. from Jadavpur University in 2013. He has also been a visiting faculty at University College of Science and Technology, University of Calcutta; AKC School of Information Technology, University of Calcutta; Jadavpur University and MAKAUT, formerly known as WBUT to name a few. Currently Prof. Bhattacharyya is doing research in Cognitive Science and Learning Management along with his registered PhD students.


Prof. Mousumi Bhattacharyya

Head, Computer Science & Engineering
Mousumi Bhattacharyya is presently working as an assistant professor in the Computer Science and Engineering Department, under Sister Nivedita University. She has completed M.Tech in Computer Technology from Jadavpur University and is pursuing Ph.D. She has more than 16 years’ experience in academics. Her interest area is Algorithm, Networking and Bioinformatics.
